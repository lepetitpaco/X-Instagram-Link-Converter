X & Instagram Link Converter

Converts links from X.com (formerly Twitter) and Instagram to privacy-friendly alternative frontends.

WHAT IT DOES

Converts links when you share or copy posts:
- X/Twitter links → fixvx.com, fixupx.com, or vxtwitter.com
- Instagram links → kkinstagram.com

HOW IT WORKS

Multiple ways to convert links:

1. In-page buttons: Buttons appear under each post on X/Twitter and Instagram. Click to copy the converted link.

2. Popup: Click the extension icon, select a conversion option.

3. Automatic (Instagram): When you click "Copy Link" on Instagram, the extension automatically converts it to kkinstagram.com.

SUPPORTED SITES

- x.com
- twitter.com
- www.instagram.com
- instagram.com

DATA COLLECTION

None. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any data. All link conversion happens locally in your browser. No tracking, no analytics, no external requests.

PERMISSIONS

- activeTab: Access current tab to convert links
- clipboardRead: Read clipboard (for Instagram automatic conversion)
- clipboardWrite: Copy converted links
- scripting: Inject conversion buttons (Manifest V3)

All permissions are used solely for link conversion functionality.

TECHNICAL

- Manifest V3 compatible
- Works with infinite scroll and dynamic content
- Automatically detects new posts as you browse
- Lightweight, no performance impact
